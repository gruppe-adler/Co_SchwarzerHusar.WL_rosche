AI DRIVING CONTROL - SCRIPT VERSION INSTALLATION GUIDE

======================================================

(1) Copy and paste folder AIDRIVINGCONTROL into your mission root folder.


(2) Insert or create this lines on DESCRIPTION.EXT:

//AI Driving Control (script version)
#include "aidrivingcontrol\cfgfunctions.hpp"


(3) Insert or create this lines on INIT.SQF (run on both clients and server):

//AI Driving Control (script version)
null = execVM "aidrivingcontrol\init.sqf";


(4) Optionally, to initiate script on a specific vehicle execute this line locally:
null = (vehicle) execVM "aidrivingcontrol\init.sqf";


Zeus vehicles are initiated automatically once steps (1), (2) and (3) are executed.
